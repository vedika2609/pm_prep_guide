# Day 1 — Evaluation Criteria (Full Rubric)

Your response will be scored on **7 dimensions** (0–10 each).  
Total = **70 points**.  
Passing FAANG threshold = **≥56** AND no category < 6.

---

# ✅ **1. Problem Framing & Scope (0–10)**  
You must show:  
- Clear and sharp articulation of the core problem  
- Real, distinct personas  
- Pain points rooted in *governed-data + AI integration*  
- Awareness of constraints (latency, governance, multi-tenancy)

**Penalties:**  
- Personas too generic  
- No pain points  
- No tangible problem articulation  

---

# ✅ **2. Product Thinking & Solution Quality (0–10)**  
Evaluates:  
- Depth & sharpness of your narrative  
- Whether your pitch feels senior & strategic  
- If the HM overview reads like a PM who owns the architecture-layer roadmap  

**Penalties:**  
- Feature dumping  
- Generic PM language  
- Buzzwords without meaning  

---

# ✅ **3. Technical Depth & System Thinking (0–10)**  
Must show:  
- Architectural thinking  
- API + routing + auth understanding  
- SLAs/SLOs  
- Tradeoffs (cost vs latency vs accuracy)

**Penalties:**  
- Too much engineering detail  
- Too little technical awareness  
- No mention of SLAs or multi-tenant constraints  

---

# ✅ **4. Metrics & Success Criteria (0–10)**  
Must include:  
- North Star (integration speed or insight speed)  
- Supporting KPIs  
- Quantified impact from your work  

**Penalties:**  
- No metrics  
- Made-up numbers  
- Weak measurement clarity  

---

# ✅ **5. Prioritization & Tradeoffs (0–10)**  
Must reflect:  
- How you navigate competing priorities  
- Why certain roadmap decisions were made  
- Stakeholder considerations  

**Penalties:**  
- No rationale  
- Everything seems equally important  
- No tradeoff statements  

---

# ✅ **6. Communication & Structure (0–10)**  
Looking for:  
- Clarity  
- Scannability  
- Executive-ready phrasing  
- Brevity without loss of depth  

**Penalties:**  
- Wordiness  
- Paragraphs too dense  
- Jargon-heavy text  

---

# ✅ **7. Leadership & Cross-Functional Influence (0–10)**  
Evaluated by:  
- Ownership level  
- Ability to align Eng, ML, Security, GTM  
- Roadmap leadership  
- Strategic influence  

**Penalties:**  
- Weak sense of ownership  
- No cross-functional mention  
- No seniority signal  

---

# ❌ Automatic Penalties (deduct 3–10 points)  
- Elevator pitch >30 words  
- Overview <280 or >350 words  
- Problem statement >80 words  
- Missing “ask / next step”  
- Missing personas  
- No metrics  
- Overly general responses
